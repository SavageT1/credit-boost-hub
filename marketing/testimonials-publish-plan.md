# Testimonials Publish Plan (Get 5 Real Testimonials)

## Collection script (text or call)
"Would you be open to sharing a short testimonial about your experience with A1 Tradelines? If yes, we may publish your first name/last initial and your comment on our website and social pages."

## Consent checklist
- Written permission (message/email)
- Confirm display format (name + initial or anonymous)
- Confirm edits allowed for grammar only

## Testimonial format template
- Name: FirstName L.
- Context: (optional short profile context)
- Quote: 1-3 sentences
- Date submitted
- Consent confirmation screenshot saved

## Placement targets
1. Homepage proof strip
2. Contact page trust block
3. Relevant blog posts
4. Google profile (when reinstated)
5. Medium/LinkedIn snippets

## Quality standards
- Real language only (no fabricated claims)
- Avoid guaranteed-outcome language
- Keep compliance-safe and realistic
