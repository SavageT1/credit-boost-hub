# The Safe Way to Buy Tradelines: A No-Hype Checklist

If you are buying tradelines, risk control matters as much as potential upside.

## Before you buy
- Review all three reports
- Define your target outcome and timeline
- Set a budget with realistic expectations

## Provider checklist
- Transparent account details
- Written posting timeline
- Clear replacement/refund policy
- No guaranteed-score claims

## After purchase
- Track reporting windows
- Keep your own utilization low
- Avoid unnecessary hard pulls
- Monitor all three bureaus

## Most common mistake
Buying multiple lines quickly without a profile diagnosis.

## Final takeaway
A safe process is methodical: diagnose, choose carefully, monitor, and adjust.
